# Terms and Conditions — AgTrader Personal Alerts

**Last updated:** April 28, 2026

## Program description

AgTrader Personal Alerts is a personal-use SMS notification service
operated by Jason Moss (the "Brand Owner"). The service sends
transactional alerts from the Brand Owner's automated trading and
monitoring systems to the Brand Owner's own verified mobile phone
number. There is no public subscription mechanism and no third-party
recipients.

## Message types

- Paper-trading account status updates
- System health alerts (Gateway up/down, paper trader up/down)
- Error notifications requiring intervention
- End-of-day P&L summaries

## Message frequency

Approximately 30 messages per month under normal operating conditions.
Higher volume is possible during system incidents.

## Costs

**Message and data rates may apply** based on the recipient's mobile
carrier plan.

## Help and opt-out

Reply **HELP** for assistance.
Reply **STOP** to opt out at any time.
The Brand Owner can also disable the originating service directly on
the server at any time.
