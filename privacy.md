# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 28, 2026

## Who this applies to

This privacy policy applies to messages sent from automated systems
operated by Jason Moss (the "Brand Owner") to the Brand Owner's own
verified mobile phone number. There are no third-party recipients and
no subscriber data is collected.

## What data is collected

The only personal data involved in the messaging service is:
- The Brand Owner's own mobile phone number (used as the message recipient)
- The Twilio Account SID and Auth Token (used to authenticate with Twilio)

No subscriber lists exist. No data is collected from any third party.
No data is shared, sold, rented, or otherwise transmitted to any
external party for any purpose, including marketing.

## How data is used

Phone numbers are used solely to deliver transactional alerts from the
Brand Owner's automated systems to the Brand Owner. Authentication
credentials are used solely to access the Twilio API.

## Retention

Messages are stored only in Twilio's standard message logs and in
local server logs operated by the Brand Owner.
